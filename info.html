How it works

Smartfridge

Our Smart-fridge will be making meals with the ingredients in your house!

Description

Our Smart-fridge can make food ideas based on your ingredients at home. It can also give you ideas for drinks such as milkshakes, homemade iced tea, etc.. The website is totally free!

Prerequisites:
You just need internet!
And of course food at home!

Getting started

Step 1 =
Go to this link: smart-fridge-app.

Step 2 =
Selcect your 'Meal type' (default = Main Course) and your 'Diet', if you have one (default = 'none').

Step 3 =
Enter your ingredient(s) and select what you have at home.

Step 4 =
You can choose, select and remove multiple ingredients. And those who you don't remove before each new search or before you go to the next step, you defenitely move to your fridge.

Step 5 =
If ready with selecting, click on: 'search recipes'. We now slect a list of food ideas with your ingredients.

Step 6 =
If you push the 'chefs button', he'll show you his 2 favorites. And if you push the 'more recipes button' you'll get 2 random recipes.

Step 7 =
Of course you can compare those recipes and switch back an forwards between our selection.
